About Melissa

If there's one thing you need to know about me it's that I absolutely, positively love food, and all kinds of it.

Growing up in a family of meat lovers it wasn't exactly easy to eat many greens or veggies. It wasn't until I looked at myself in the mirror and decided that my diet needed to change, or else I was going to go down a bad, or should I say, round path. It wasn't only my physical appearance I needed to work on, it was my energy levels and overall mood that needed to change. I had been completely oblivious about nutrition and diet for over 20 years.

It wasn't until I began educating myself on the importance of organic healthy eating that my mind became clearer and my waist became smaller. I think like a lot of people, I didn't know that healthy food could be enjoyable to eat. And that's where the inspiration to start eating leaves came from: to let average, everyday people, like me, know that you can make flavorful, yet affordable food that is good for you, while feeling good about yourself in the process.

Creating these recipes also inspired me to open a little shop that offered my all time favourite vegan snacks and desserts. After months of trial and error sessions in the kitchen, I finally decided on eight different products that are not only super delicious, but healthy and guilt-free to eat as well. Made with organic ingredients and natural sweeteners, you won't believe they're good for you.

With that being said, I hope you also stay a while, browse through some of my recipes and make something for yourself and others in the process. You'll be amazed at what you can gain by eating leaves.

Feel free to connect with me through Facebook, Twitter, Google Plus or Instagram - or send me an email at info@eatingleaves.com with any questions or special orders. I can't wait to eat well with you! :)

- Melissa xo
